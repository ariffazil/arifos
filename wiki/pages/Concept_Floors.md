## Why Floors Exist

arifOS is grounded in a **thermodynamic theory of intelligence**:

- **Axiom 1 (Landauer):** Truth has a price. Cheap outputs are likely false. `P(truth | energy=0) = 0`
- **Axiom 2 (Scar-Weight):** Authority ∝ Suffering Capacity. `W_scar(AI) = 0` → AI cannot be sovereign.
- **Axiom 3 (Anti-Entropy):** Clarity requires work. Governance IS the cost of local entropy decrease.
- **Axiom 4 (Genius):** Wisdom is multiplicative. `G = A × P × X × E²` — any zero factor collapses G to 0.

The 13 Floors **enforce all four axioms** at runtime.
