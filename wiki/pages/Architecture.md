# The Trinity Architecture

**The Trinity Architecture (ΔΩΨ)** is the fundamental structural paradigm of arifOS, forged to separate the Human, the Law, and the Implementation.

## Separation of Concerns
1. **HUMAN (Δ The Body / Epistemic Layer)**: `arif-fazil.com`
   - Represents identity, scars, values, and the founder's biography.
   - Why: AI must know *who* it serves to provide context-aware, empathetic governance (e.g., the Abah Check).
2. **THEORY (Ψ The Soul / Authority Layer)**: `apex.arif-fazil.com`
   - Houses the immutable 13 Constitutional Floors (F1-F13), the Three Pillars (Φ, ∑, λ), and Verdicts.
   - Why: Law must stand independent of personal narrative and implementation details.
3. **APPS (Ω The Mind / Implementation Layer)**: `arifos_arif-fazil.com`
   - Contains the L1–L7 Stack, MCP tools, SDK, and executable code.
   - Why: Implementation changes frequently; law does not.

## The Veto Layer — 888 Judge
The 888 Judge holds ultimate sovereign authority (F13). The human can override any machine SEAL, modify the constitution, or shut down the system.
