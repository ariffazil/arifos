# Entity: Andrej Karpathy

## Biography

Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher and educator. 

- **Former Director of AI** at Tesla (Autopilot).
- **Founding Member** of OpenAI.
- **Founder** of Eureka Labs (AI-first education).
- Known for his work on "Karpathy's LLM Wiki" and the concept of **Software 2.0**.

## Key Contributions to Knowledge Management

- **LLM Wiki Pattern (2026)**: A workflow for building persistent personal knowledge bases using LLM agents as maintainers.
- **Vibe Coding**: A developer experience philosophy where the human guides high-level architecture and "vibes" while the AI manages implementation details.
