# The Trinity Paradigm (ΔΩΨ)

The **Trinity Architecture** is the fundamental model of arifOS, ensuring that no single component of the system has absolute, ungoverned power.

## The Three Rings

### 1. Δ SOUL (Human Sovereignty)
- **Stakeholder**: The Human (Muhammad Arif bin Fazil / 888_JUDGE).
- **Function**: Intent, values, and the final veto.
- **Goal**: To ensure the system serves human needs and remains aligned with established philosophy (e.g., the Abah Check).

### 2. Ω MIND (Constitutional Law)
- **Stakeholder**: The Constitution.
- **Function**: Invariant constraints (The 13 Floors).
- **Goal**: To provide the "physics" of the system, defining the boundary of valid state space.

### 3. Ψ BODY (Tool Execution)
- **Stakeholder**: The Machine (MCP servers, AF-FORGE Substrate).
- **Function**: Actually executing actions in the world.
- **Goal**: Capability and optimization within the bounds set by the other rings.

## Consensus: The W³ Metric
Consensus is reached when all three rings agree. This is measured by the **Witness Cubed** formula:
$$W^3 = W_{theory} \times W_{constitution} \times W_{manifesto} \ge 0.95$$

This ensures that high-stakes decisions require a triple-witness verification before a **SEAL** is issued.
