# Concept: Architecture (The Trinity ΔΩΨ)

arifOS is built upon the **Trinity Architecture**, a three-layered governance model designed to ensure that AI agents operate within human-defined ethical, physical, and logical bounds.

## 🏛️ The Three Pillars (ΔΩΨ)

1.  **Δ Mind (AGI - Intelligence)**: Logic, reasoning, and multi-source synthesis. Focuses on clarity, uncertainty quantification, and hallucination prevention.
2.  **Ω Heart (ASI - Safety)**: Ethical critique, consequence simulation, and empathy. Acts as the adversarial layer to the Mind.
3.  **Ψ Soul (APEX - Governance)**: Sovereign judgment, final verification, and immutable auditing. Houses the Constitutional Kernel (CCC).

## 🌉 The Archi-Bridge (AAA/BBB/CCC)

The system enforces a physical and logical separation between the user-facing interface and the governing kernel.

-   **AAA (Application Layer)**: Mutable, user-facing interfaces (CLI, Web, MCP).
-   **BBB (Protocol Bridge)**: The audited channel through which intent travels.
-   **CCC (Constitutional Kernel)**: The immutable, rule-enforcing Metabolic Core.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Separation of Powers**: Tools like `arifos_forge` are strictly execution-only and require a **SEAL** verdict from `arifos_judge` to operate. This ensures that the kernel evaluates *risk* before the machine performs *work*.
