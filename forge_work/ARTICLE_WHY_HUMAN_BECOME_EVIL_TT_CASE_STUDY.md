For 50 years, Sarawak received 5% royalty while the federal government captured the rest.

In 2017, Sarawak established PETROS — its own oil and gas company. In 2024, Sarawak asserted PETROS as the state's sole gas aggregator. The constitutional fight began.

| Legal posture | Defend PDA 1974 primacy | Propose out-of-court federal-state compact |
| Negotiation style | "Awaiting clarity" (passive/reactive) | Proactive partnership proposal |
| Public narrative | "Defending national energy security" and "dividends may drop" | "Architect of new federal-state compact" |

**OBS:** TT has been CEO throughout the entire PETRONAS-PETROS escalation.
**DER:** At every observable junction, TT chose litigation, delay, and monopoly defense over genuine partnership.
**INT:** TT is the human invariant executing a consistent strategy: protect the federal petroleum monopoly, even at the cost of federal-state cohesion.
