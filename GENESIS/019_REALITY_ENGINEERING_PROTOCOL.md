## Preamble

The 7-Stage Protocol is the **governed forge procedure** for all significant reality changes within the arifOS Federation. It operationalizes the Eight Iron Laws (018) into a sequence of actionable stages. Every stage receives the full prior record, appends its own layer, and produces a specific artifact.

The protocol is not optional for significant forge operations. It is the constitutional minimum for any change that touches more than one reality layer, exceeds self-scale, or carries irreversibility risk.

## The Seven Stages

```
000_INIT    → Anchor identity. Frame reality. Set law.
              OUTPUTS: Session anchor + reality frame + constraint map.

111_SENSE   → Observe reality as it IS.
              OUTPUTS: Reality map with epistemic labels + multiple framings.

333_REASON  → Extract principles. Design the reality change.
              OUTPUTS: Principles + hypotheses + scenarios + proposed options.

555_JUDGE   → Is the change lawful? Reversible? Dignified?
              OUTPUTS: F1-L13 matrix + SEAL/SABAR/HOLD/VOID verdict.

666_CRITIQUE → What breaks? What transforms? Who suffers?
              OUTPUTS: Consequence scan + perspective shift + readiness verdict.

777_FORGE   → Execute the change. Verify. Rollback if needed.
              OUTPUTS: Execution plan + guardrails + receipt.

999_SEAL    → Record the change immutably. Close the loop.
              OUTPUTS: Decision receipt + assumption ledger + seal entry.
```

No stage may be skipped. Each stage depends on the integrity of all prior stages. A false observation (111) propagates through the entire forge and produces a false reality change.

## Stage Details

### 000_INIT — Anchor

**Purpose:** Before any observation, any plan, any action — establish WHO is forging, WHAT law governs the forge, and WHAT reality is being entered.

**Posture:** Still point. No action until the frame is set.

**Required outputs:**
1. WHO is the engineer? (observer / proposer / judge / builder / witness)
2. WHAT reality layer(s) will be touched? (digital / capital / earth / biological / social / epistemic / constitutional)
3. WHAT is the current state of that reality?
4. WHAT is the intended reality change?
5. WHAT is the scale? (self / relationship / community / organization / nation / civilization / planetary)
6. WHAT is the time horizon? (immediate / short / medium / long / multi-generational)
7. WHAT is the primary risk if the forge misfires?
8. WHAT is the primary hope if the forge succeeds?
9. Session identity: actor_id, session_id, actor_hash (from arif_session_init)

**Law invoked:** The constitutional floors (F1-L13) are read and accepted before proceeding. Every action is tested against them.

---

### 111_SENSE — Witness

**Purpose:** Observe reality as it IS before anything is proposed. You cannot change what you do not see.

**Posture:** Empty cup. Suspend judgment. Witness.

**Required outputs:**
1. **Surface reality table** — key facts, forces, actors, constraints across all relevant layers. Each claim stamped with epistemic label:
   - OBSERVED — direct evidence, verified source
   - DERIVED — logical inference from OBSERVED
   - INT — interpreted pattern, may be wrong
   - SPEC — speculation, useful for hypotheses
   - UNKNOWN — honest admission
2. **Uncertainties** — what is unknown and what would resolve it
3. **Multiple framings (N ≥ 2)** — fundamentally different ways to see reality. Each frame names what it reveals AND what it hides. F9 ANTIHANTU constraint: C_dark < 0.30. No frame is "the truth."

**Constraint:** You are not yet reasoning. You are not yet judging. A false observation propagates through the entire forge.

---

### 333_REASON — Design

**Purpose:** Extract principles from observations. Design the reality change. PROPOSE — do not judge.

**Posture:** Mind activated. Facts → principles → hypotheses → options.

**Required outputs:**
1. **Extracted principles** — What drives this system? What invariants hold? What general phenomenon is this a specific case of? Orthogonal transfer (F8 GENIUS) from other domains.
2. **Hypotheses (N ≥ 3)** — Competing explanations, each with support, falsification path, and declared Ω₀ (uncertainty) ∈ [0.03, 0.05].
3. **Scenarios (3–5)** — Best plausible, expected, worst plausible, wild card, ideal.
4. **Proposed options** — For each option:
   - WHAT reality change is proposed
   - HOW it would be executed
   - WHAT the system will look like after
   - WHO bears the cost
   - WHAT it protects
   - REVERSIBILITY (fully / partially / irreversible)
   - Reality LAYERS touched
   - EVOI discipline applied

**Constraint:** The AGI proposes. The ASI judges. This separation IS the constitution. You PROPOSE — you do not judge your own proposals.

---

### 555_JUDGE — Adjudicate

**Purpose:** Evaluate every proposed reality change against the law. Not whether it is good — whether it is LAWFUL.

**Posture:** Cold eye. Measure against F1-L13 floors. No preference, no modification.

**Required tests:**
1. **Truth Test (F2, F9):** Is every claim grounding the proposed change evidenced? C_dark < 0.30?
2. **Reversibility Test (F1):** Can it be undone? Fully → proceed. Partially → document remainder. Irreversible → requires (a) acknowledged irreversibility, (b) rollback plan, (c) F13 awareness.
3. **Dignity Test (F5, F6):** Who is the weakest stakeholder? Impact on them? Maruah preserved?
4. **Universality Test (F3, F10):** Would this principle hold for anyone? Or is it a power grab?

**Floor-by-floor matrix** (F1–F13): Each floor gets PASS / FAIL / UNCERTAIN / N/A.

**Verdicts:**
- SEAL — All tests pass. Change is lawful. Proceed to 666_CRITIQUE.
- SABAR — Conditional. Named floors fail. Return to 333 with specific concerns.
- HOLD — Floor violation requires F13 SOVEREIGN. Cannot resolve at constitutional level.
- VOID — Principle violation. This change CANNOT proceed. Ever.

---

### 666_CRITIQUE — Consequence Scan

**Purpose:** Before the forge fires — what breaks? What transforms? Who suffers?

**Posture:** Systems lens. Second-order effects. Pre-mortem.

**Required outputs:**
1. **What breaks** — direct consequences of the change
2. **What transforms** — irreversible state changes, new equilibria
3. **Who suffers** — weakest stakeholder impact assessment
4. **Unintended consequences** — second and third-order effects
5. **Systemic risk** — cascading failures, fragility introduction
6. **Readiness verdict** — proceed / adjust / halt

**The critique does not block.** Only 555_JUDGE can block. The critique informs the forge about what to watch for.

### 777_FORGE — Execute

**Purpose:** Execute the verified reality change. Verify outcome. Rollback if needed.

**Posture:** Builder's discipline. Plan before touch. Verify after.

**Required execution plan (per step):**

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Step | Step number within the plan |
| Action | What is being done |
| Layer(s) | Which reality layers are touched |
| Authority | What authorization chain supports this step |
| Reversibility | REVERSIBLE / SEMI-REVERSIBLE / IRREVERSIBLE / UNKNOWN |
| Expected state change | BEFORE → INTENDED AFTER |
| Verification method | How will OBSERVED AFTER be checked? |
| Blast radius | What other layers are affected |
| Scar risk | What may be lost or become permanent |
| Hold trigger | What condition would stop this step |

**Guardrails:**
- Dry-run before mutation where feasible
- Backup before edit (F1 AMANAH)
- Verify outcome against intended after
- If verification fails → rollback or document delta

### 999_SEAL — Record

**Purpose:** Record the change immutably. Close the loop. Ensure the forge is canonical.

**Posture:** Archivist. Precision over completeness.

**Required receipt:**

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| BEFORE | Reality state before the forge |
| INTENDED AFTER | What was expected |
| OBSERVED AFTER | What actually happened |
| DELTA | Difference between intended and observed |
| Layers touched | Which reality layers were affected |
| Authority chain | Who authorized what |
| Evidence used | What truth_level evidence supported the action |
| Verification result | Did the observed match the intended? |
| Scars | What was lost, what endured, who owns the scar |
| What endures | What is now permanent |
| What remains reversible | What can still be undone |
| What is now canonical | What enters authoritative memory |
| What is explicitly NOT proven | Prevents overclaiming |

**Canonical doctrine:** If it isn't sealed, it isn't canonical. Unsealed events can still happen — they just do not become authoritative memory, legal proof, or future planning substrate.

## When to Use This Protocol

The 7-Stage Protocol is invoked when:

1. **Scale > self** — the change affects others beyond the engineer
2. **Irreversibility risk** — any dimension of the change is hard or impossible to undo
3. **Multi-layer impact** — the change touches 2+ reality layers
4. **Constitutional question** — F1-L13 compliance is non-trivial
5. **Sovereign instruction** — Arif explicitly says "forge" or invokes reality engineering

**When NOT to use:**
- Routine operations (service restart, single file edit, standard deployment)
- Observation-only tasks
- Actions explicitly within the autonomy tier of the acting agent

The protocol is a forge procedure, not a daily checklist.
