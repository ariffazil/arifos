arifOS is the constitutional kernel of the federation. It structures judgment,
governance, memory, audit, and sealing paths. It does not own domain truth,
operator UX, or deployment substrate.

## Owns

- Constitutional Floors F1-F13 and their enforcement semantics.
- Canonical governance pipeline: session, sense, evidence, mind, route, reply,
  memory, critique, gateway, ops, judge, forge, vault.
- `888_JUDGE`, `999_SEAL` eligibility checks, and VAULT999 audit/receipt paths.
- Canonical tool registry and public arifOS MCP contract.
- Federation contracts that define cross-repo authority and routing.
- Memory governance contracts, including recall, audit, escalation, and retention
  rules.

## Does Not Own

- GEOX geoscience, petrophysics, seismic, map, or physical Earth calculations.
- WEALTH capital allocation engines, portfolio math, or investment memo logic.
- AAA cockpit UX, identity screens, A2A operator console, or session UI.
- A-FORGE Docker/compose, ingress, release assembly, or host deployment scripts,
  except for arifOS-owned deploy manifests under `deploy/`.
- WELL biological, human-readiness, or substrate-vitality domain logic.
- Hermes/OpenClaw bot identity or Telegram behavior.

## Imports From

- GEOX: Earth evidence envelopes, prospect evidence, map/subsurface artifacts.
- WEALTH: capital viability, risk, EMV, and decision memo artifacts.
- WELL: readiness/substrate signals and health envelopes.
- AAA: operator/session identity, A2A context, cockpit state references.
- A-FORGE: deployment status, build provenance, release health, rollback notes.
- VAULT999/Postgres/Qdrant/Redis: persistence, memory, vector recall, ledger state.

## Exports To

- All federation repos: floor policy, verdict semantics, audit contracts, and
  federation authority map.
- AAA: governed session state, health, tool registry summaries, and operator
  verdict context.
- A-FORGE: SEAL/HOLD execution gates, deployment constraints, rollback
  requirements, and vault receipts.
- GEOX/WEALTH/WELL: governance envelope requirements and claim-state vocabulary.
- Hermes/OpenClaw/Codex/Gemini/Claude/Kimi/OpenCode/Copilot: machine-readable
  operating limits and escalation boundaries.
