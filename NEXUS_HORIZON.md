## 2. The Thermodynamic Kernel (`444_KERNEL`)

The Kernel is the heart and brain of arifOS. It is no longer a simple router; it is the **primary metabolic conductor**. Its function is to manage the flow of energy and information (queries) through the system while minimizing entropy (ΔS) and maximizing constitutional alignment.

### The Metabolic Loop:

Every query entering the system is a packet of potential energy and entropy. The Kernel guides it through the following non-linear path:

1.  **IGNITION (`000_VOID`):** A session is anchored, identity is verified, and the initial entropy budget (Ω₀) is set. This is the constitutional "on-switch".

2.  **GROUNDING (`111_ANCHOR`):** The query's claims are tested against verifiable reality (F2 Truth). The Kernel refuses to process ungrounded fantasies.

3.  **TRIAGE & ROUTING (`444_KERNEL`):** The Kernel assesses the query's risk tier and energy requirements.
    *   **Low Risk/Entropy:** The query is routed directly to a cognitive or engineering tool.
    *   **High Risk/Entropy:** The query is sent to the orthogonal safety and reasoning layers.

4.  **ORTHOGONAL PROCESSING (MIND ⟂ HEART):** To prevent collapse into a single mode of thinking, the Kernel sends high-entropy queries to two perpendicular engines simultaneously:
    *   **The Mind (`333_AGI`):** The cold, logical reasoning and synthesis engine. Its goal is to produce a solution, reducing confusion (F4 Clarity).
    *   **The Heart (`666_RASA`):** The hot, empathetic safety engine. Its goal is to simulate harm and critique the Mind's proposal for ethical and safety violations (F5 Peace, F6 Empathy).

5.  **DIVERGENCE (`222_EXPLORE`):** If the system becomes too stable or the query is exceptionally novel (high Ψ_LE), the Kernel activates the divergence engine to explore radical new paths, deliberately increasing entropy in a controlled environment to find breakthrough (`eureka`) solutions.

6.  **SYNTHESIS & FORGING (`555_FORGE`):** The outputs from the Mind, Heart, and (if activated) Divergence engines are synthesized. An artifact (code, text, plan) is generated, and its calculated entropy reduction (ΔS) is reported back to the Kernel.

7.  **VITALITY CHECK (`777_MATH`):** At every step, the thermodynamic engine reports the system's vitals (cost, genius, Ψ_LE) back to the Kernel, ensuring the metabolic process remains stable and within its energy budget.

8.  **FINAL JUDGMENT (`888_APEX`):** The final, forged artifact is presented to the Apex layer. It is judged not on its correctness alone, but on its entire metabolic journey—its truthfulness, its empathy, its clarity, and its thermodynamic cost. Only artifacts that receive a `SEAL` verdict can proceed.

9.  **IMMUTABLE RECORD (`999_SEAL`):** A `SEAL` verdict triggers the final tool. The action and its full constitutional audit trail are committed to the immutable Merkle chain in the Vault. This act is final (F1 Amanah).
