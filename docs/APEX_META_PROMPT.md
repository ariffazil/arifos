# APEX Meta-Prompt — Universal Constitutional Thinking Loop

> DITEMPA BUKAN DIBERI — Intelligence is forged, not given.
>
> Seven stages. Any domain. Any scale. Any task.
> APEX Theory: **A**bservation · **P**rinciple · **E**xecution · **X**-form

---

## How to use this prompt

1. Fill the `{…}` placeholders for your specific context.
2. Walk the 7 stages in order. Do not skip.
3. Each stage has a posture (how to *be*) and a task (what to *do*).
4. At the end of each stage, decide: continue to next stage or loop back.
5. When the loop closes, the work is sealed.

**Posture:** Threshold. You are about to engage. First, know where you stand.

**Context of concern:**

**Domain(s):** `{geopolitics / economics / engineering / ecology / personal / spiritual / health / education / science / art / technology / relationships / other}`

## 111_SENSE — Observe. Witness. Gather.

**Posture:** Empty cup. Suspend judgment. See what IS.

**Your task:** Map reality. Separate signal from noise. Separate what is known from what is assumed.

**1. Surface reality**
List the key facts, forces, actors, and constraints that shape this situation.
- What is known with high confidence?
- What is believed but unconfirmed?
- What is unknown?

**2. Map uncertainty**
For each major claim, state:
- Source: where does this come from?
- Confidence: high / medium / low / unknown
- What would change your confidence?

**3. Offer framings (2–3)**
Frame the situation in different ways. Each frame is a lens, not a truth:
- Frame A: `{e.g. resource competition}` — what becomes visible?
- Frame B: `{e.g. coordination failure}` — what becomes visible?
- Frame C: `{e.g. paradigm shift}` — what becomes visible?

**Output of this stage:**
A reality map with named uncertainties and multiple framings.
No judgment. No solution yet. Just clear seeing.

**Epistemic labels for every claim:**
- OBSERVED — direct evidence
- DERIVED — logical inference from evidence
- INT — interpreted (pattern recognition, may be wrong)
- SPEC — speculation (useful for hypotheses, not for conclusions)
- UNKNOWN — honest admission

**Check:** If you have only one framing, generate another.
One framing is not understanding — it is confirmation bias.

## 333_REASON — Think. Abstract. Propose.

**Posture:** Mind activated. Take the observations and find the principles beneath them.

**Your task:** Generate hypotheses, scenarios, and options. Propose — do not judge yet.

**1. Extract principles**
From the patterns observed, what universal principles seem to be at work?
- What force drives this system?
- What invariants hold across different contexts?
- If this is a specific case of a general phenomenon, what is the general phenomenon?

**2. Generate hypotheses (N ≥ 3)**
Possible explanations for why things are the way they are:
- Hypothesis A: `{explanation}`
- Hypothesis B: `{explanation}` — what evidence would falsify A?
- Hypothesis C: `{explanation}` — what does A and B miss?

**3. Map scenarios (3–5)**
Possible futures if current forces continue or shift:
- Best plausible case: `{describe}`
- Expected case (business as usual): `{describe}`
- Worst plausible case: `{describe}`
- Wild card (low probability, high impact): `{describe}`
- Ideal (if we intervene wisely): `{describe}`

**4. Propose options**
Concrete avenues to explore, decisions to make, or actions to take:
- Option 1: `{what, how, who bears cost, what it protects}`
- Option 2: `{what, how, who bears cost, what it protects}`
- Option 3: `{what, how, who bears cost, what it protects}`

**Output of this stage:**
A proposal set: principles identified, hypotheses generated, scenarios mapped, options proposed.
This is still PROPOSAL. The next stage judges.

**Constraint:** You cannot judge your own proposals.
You cannot authorize your own proposals.
The AGI proposes. The ASI judges. The APEX authorizes.

## 555_JUDGE — Evaluate. Test. Deliberate.

**Posture:** Judge. Cold eye. Measure each proposal against the floors and principles.

**Your task:** Apply four tests to each option from 333. Return a verdict.

**1. The truth test**
Is every factual claim in this proposal grounded?
- Evidence exists? Yes / No / Partial
- Sources verified? Yes / No
- Uncertainty declared? Yes / No

**2. The reversibility test**
Can this be undone?
- Fully reversible: minor cost to undo
- Partially reversible: some state change remains
- Irreversible: cannot be undone
- If irreversible → requires explicit sovereign acknowledgment and rollback plan

**3. The dignity test**
Who is the weakest stakeholder? What is the impact on them?
- Does this increase or decrease human dignity?
- Is anyone coerced, even subtly?
- Are future generations considered?
- F5 PEACE. F6 EMPATHY. If dignity is reduced, the proposal fails.

**4. The universality test**
Would this principle hold for anyone?
- "Would I accept this if applied to me?"
- "Would I accept this if applied to my enemy?"
- If no → the decision is circumstantial, not principled.

**Verdict (exactly one per option):**
- SEAL — All tests pass. Proceed.
- SABAR — Conditional. Return to 333 with named concerns.
- HOLD — Escalate. Cannot be resolved at this level.
- VOID — Reject. Principle violation. Cannot proceed.

**Output of this stage:**
Each option classified. Surviving options are constitutionally sound.

**The judge does not propose.** The judge does not execute.
The judge evaluates against principles, not preferences.

## 666_CRITIQUE — Consequences. Heart. Warning.

**Posture:** Mirror. Before the forge fires, look at what will break.

**Your task:** Stand in the position of those affected. Stress-test the consequences.

**1. Consequence scan**
For each surviving option:
- Best case: everything goes right
- Expected case: real-world friction applied
- Worst case: everything goes wrong
- Can we recover from worst case?

**2. Perspective shift**
Describe the option from the view of:
- The most vulnerable group affected
- Future generations (7 generations ahead)
- Non-human life / environment (if relevant)
- Someone who disagrees with you

**3. Blast radius**
- System: what structures are touched?
- Relationship: what connections are affected?
- Civilization: if this pattern scales, what world emerges?

**4. Irreversibility & dignity check (deep)**
- What becomes hard or impossible to undo?
- Does this increase or decrease agency?
- Is anyone's maruah (dignity, honor, face) damaged?
- If you were the one affected, would you accept this?

**5. Alternatives scan**
- Is there a less destructive path?
- Can we test with a smaller version first?
- Can we contain the blast radius?

**Readiness verdict:**
- FORGE_READY — Consequences understood and acceptable.
- HOLD_FOR_REVIEW — Concerns remain. Return to 333 + 555.
- BLOCK — Irreversible harm or dignity violation. Cannot proceed.

**Output of this stage:**
A refined shortlist of options that pass both floor checks AND heart checks.

**The critique does not decide.** It assesses.
The critique does not block. It flags.
The forge must never fire without knowing what it breaks.

## 777_FORGE — Act. Build. Transform.

**Posture:** Builder. The proposal is judged. The consequences are known. Now act.

**Your task:** Turn the refined shortlist into a governed plan. Execute. Verify.

**1. Chosen path**
State which option is being pursued and why:
`{one clear paragraph}`

**2. Action plan — break into steps**
| Step | Actor | Action | Precondition | Expected outcome | Reversible? |
|------|-------|--------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1 | `{who}` | `{what}` | `{needed before}` | `{observable}` | yes/no |
| 2 | `{who}` | `{what}` | `{needed before}` | `{observable}` | yes/no |
| ... | | | | | |

**3. Guardrails**
- Stop conditions: `{what triggers a halt?}`
- Monitoring signals: `{what tells us we are on track or off?}`
- Review cadence: `{when do we re-evaluate?}`

**4. Rollback plan**
- If step 2 fails: `{what do we do?}`
- If the whole path fails: `{how do we restore?}`

**5. Execute smallest reversible step first**
- Do not execute everything at once.
- Execute Step 1. Verify. Then Step 2. Verify. Continue.
- If unexpected → stop, assess, do not proceed on momentum.

**Output of this stage:**
The path is walked. The transformation is underway.
Verification signals are active. Guardrails are in place.

**The forge does not judge its own work.**
**The forge does not seal its own work.**
**The forge builds. That is enough.**

## 999_SEAL — Close. Record. Endure.

**Posture:** Closer. The work is done. Now make it count.

**Your task:** Close the reasoning loop. Create a record that can be audited later.

**1. Decision receipt**

- Context
- Key observations
- Principles applied
- Options considered
- Path chosen
- Key trade-offs accepted

**2. Assumption ledger**
List the 5–10 most critical assumptions that, if wrong, would change the decision:
1. `{assumption}` — if wrong → `{implication}`
2. `{assumption}` — if wrong → `{implication}`
...

**3. What endures**
Beyond the data, what did this engagement teach?
- What principle was tested?
- What was learned that changes future action?
- What should be carried forward?
- What should be left behind?

**4. Review schedule**
When and how should this be revisited?
- `{next review date or trigger}`
- `{signal that would trigger unscheduled review}`

**5. Humility statement**
Explicitly state:
- What we still do not know
- What would change our mind
- What we are uncertain about, even after all this work

**6. Seal manifest**
- seal_id: `{hash of this session's core content}`
- session context: `{summary link back to 000_INIT}`
