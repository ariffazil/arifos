# WAJIB VOCABULARY — arifOS Federation | The 33 Core Terms

This document defines the 33 canonical operating terms of arifOS. They are divided into 3 domains of 11 terms each.

---

## A. SUBSTRATE (Δ) — Machine Reality

* **Engineering Law**: *SUBSTRATE carries state. SUBSTRATE does not judge. SUBSTRATE does not reason.*

|  # | Term | Meaning | Never Mix With |
| -: | ---- | ------- | -------------- |
|  1 | **SUBSTRATE** | The machine layer that carries runtime state: server, process, schema, dependency, transport | Constitution or Cognition |
|  2 | **STATE** | Current measurable condition of a system | Verdict |
|  3 | **SCHEMA** | Formal shape of accepted input/output | Doctrine |
|  4 | **TRANSPORT** | How messages move: HTTP, MCP, A2A, stdio, webhook | Authority |
|  5 | **RUNTIME** | The running service/process/version/environment | Intelligence |
|  6 | **DEPENDENCY** | External/internal service required for function | Sovereign authority |
|  7 | **TOOL** | Callable capability exposed to agents | Agent |
|  8 | **EXECUTION** | Actual machine-side action/change | Judgment |
|  9 | **ARTIFACT** | Produced object: file, patch, report, result, log bundle | Receipt |
| 10 | **TRACE** | Step-by-step technical path of what happened | Truth by itself |
| 11 | **RETAK** | Substrate degraded/cracked: schema drift, dependency unknown, runtime unstable | BANGANG or VOID |

## B. CONSTITUTION (Ω) — Governance Reality

* **Engineering Law**: *CONSTITUTION bounds action. CONSTITUTION does not execute. CONSTITUTION does not hallucinate evidence.*

|  # | Term | Meaning | Never Mix With |
| -: | ---- | ------- | -------------- |
| 12 | **CONSTITUTION** | Governance layer that bounds action through rules, floors, authority, and veto | Machine health |
| 13 | **FLOOR** | Non-negotiable constraint such as truth, reversibility, audit, human veto | Suggestion |
| 14 | **AUTHORITY** | Who/what is allowed to approve, hold, act, or veto | Capability |
| 15 | **LEASE** | Temporary bounded permission to act within scope | Permanent ownership |
| 16 | **VERDICT** | Governance decision: SEAL, HOLD, SABAR, VOID | Emotion or confidence |
| 17 | **SEAL** | Approved under stated evidence, authority, and scope | "Done", "true forever", or F13 |
| 18 | **HOLD** | Stop because evidence, authority, reversibility, or clarity is missing | Failure |
| 19 | **SABAR** | Slow down; proceed only after cooling, review, or staged restraint | HOLD or SEAL |
| 20 | **VOID** | Invalid/blocked due to contradiction, unsafe action, deception, or unauthorized path | HOLD |
| 21 | **AMANAH** | Governance is trustworthy: bounded, honest, reversible where possible | BIJAKSANA |
| 22 | **F13** | Arif’s sovereign veto and final ratification layer | SEAL, model confidence, or tool output |

## C. COGNITION (Ψ) — Intelligence Reality

* **Engineering Law**: *COGNITION reduces uncertainty. COGNITION does not authorize. COGNITION does not execute.*

|  # | Term | Meaning | Never Mix With |
| -: | ---- | ------- | -------------- |
| 23 | **COGNITION** | Intelligence layer that interprets, reasons, critiques, synthesizes, metabolizes | Tool execution |
| 24 | **INTENT** | What the human/agent is trying to achieve | Command |
| 25 | **EVIDENCE** | Support for a claim or action | Confidence |
| 26 | **INFERENCE** | Reasoned conclusion from evidence | Fact |
| 27 | **UNCERTAINTY** | Known limit, ambiguity, or missing evidence | Weakness |
| 28 | **METABOLIZE** | Convert result/error/scar into learning and improved future routing | Summarize |
| 29 | **BANGANG** | Cognition incoherent: confused, overconfident, contradictory, ungrounded | Substrate error or insult |
| 30 | **BIJAK** | Locally smart/useful reasoning, but not fully governed | BIJAKSANA or SEAL |
| 31 | **BIJAKSANA** | Wise cognition: humble, evidence-aware, bounded, consequence-aware | Smartness alone |
| 32 | **HANTU** | Ghost cognition: fluent output with no valid evidence, authority, or substrate anchor | Creativity |
| 33 | **MARUAH** | Human dignity boundary inside cognition and governance | Sentiment only |

## 5. Critical Rules of Separation

* **Never call RETAK a verdict.**
* **Never call BANGANG a substrate error.**
* **Never call BIJAKSANA a SEAL.**
* **Never let SEAL impersonate F13.**
* **Never let HANTU pass as evidence.**
* **Never let BIJAK pretend to be BIJAKSANA.**

## 6. Term Admission & Pruning

All canonical terms are subject to:
* **CANON_GATE**: No term enters the 33 unless it names a real operational distinction and changes machine behavior, governance judgment, or intelligence diagnosis.
* **CANON_GARBAGE_COLLECTION**: Terms not affecting routing, judgment, diagnosis, execution, or audit are demoted to metaphor.
