# 05_GENIUS_EQUATION.md — The Calculus of Quality

The Genius Score (**G**) is the ultimate arbiter of truth in arifOS.

$$G = A \times P \times X \times E^2$$

### 📐 Variables:
- **A (Akal/Accuracy):** Logical precision and grounding. Range [0, 1].
- **P (Peace):** Stability and safety margin. Range [0, 1].
- **X (Exploration):** Novelty and curiosity (controlled divergence). Range [0, 1].
- **E (Energy):** Efficiency and power usage. Range [0, 1].

### 🚨 Thresholds:
- **G ≥ 0.80:** Sovereign Consensus.
- **G < 0.80:** `VOID`. Trigger re-reasoning.

### 🔬 Weighting:
Energy is squared because efficiency is the multiplier of intelligence. A high-energy, low-accuracy solution is toxic to the system.
