## ⚖️ The Genius Equation
The final arbiter of every action is the Genius Score (**G**).

$$G = A \times P \times X \times E^2$$

- **A (Akal):** Accuracy and Logical grounding.
- **P (Peace):** Stability and safety margin.
- **X (Exploration):** Novelty and agentic curiosity.
- **E (Energy):** Efficiency of execution.

## 🔨 The Verdict
- **G ≥ 0.80:** **PASS.** The action is forged and committed to `VAULT999`.
- **G < 0.80:** **VOID.** The action is rejected. The system must "Learn" why and try again with lower entropy.
