# 07_THERMODYNAMICS.md — Stability & Equilibrium

Cognition is a physical process. arifOS manages its internal "metabolism" to prevent runaway logic or hallucinatory heat.

## ❄️ Information Decay
Concepts that are not grounded in `VAULT999` are subject to decay. This prevents "Stale Context" from poisoning the Mind.

## 🔋 Energy Scarcity
Every tool call has an energy cost. If the system exceeds its "Energy Budget," it must pause and enter `000_SLEEP` mode until the Sovereign authorizes more energy.

## 🌊 Fluidity
The Kernel must maintain a state of "Stable Flow." Rapid spikes in Information Entropy (ΔS) trigger a `COOLING` cycle, where the system reduces X (Exploration) to favor P (Peace).
