## Newton's Laws in arifOS

### 1. Law of Inertia
> A cognitive state remains in context unless acted upon by new intent, evidence, critique, or judgment.

`arif_memory_recall` preserves cognitive momentum. Context carries forward unless corrected, routed, or sealed.

### 2. Law of Force
> Intent force = constitutional mass × permitted reasoning acceleration.

The stronger the intent, the more mass (13-tool surface) and depth (stage chain) required before action propagates.

```text
Low-risk observe    → light governance mass
Critical seal/execute → full chain: sense → mind → heart → judge → vault
```

No lompat stage. No skip.

### 3. Law of Action-Reaction
> Every action must model its opposite accountability before propagation.

`arif_heart_critique` models harm before `arif_judge_deliberate` seals judgment. Most AI systems react after harm. arifOS models reaction before action becomes consequence.
