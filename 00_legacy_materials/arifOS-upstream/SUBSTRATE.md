## 💡 Simple Stack Breakdown (Plain Language)

### The Jobs
1. **SQL (The Truth):** Safe, permanent storage. The "Memory" that doesn't change unless you tell it to.
2. **Python (The Smart):** The engine that does the heavy lifting, math, and AI reasoning.
3. **JSON (The Courier):** The universal package used to ship data between the Brain and the Organs.
4. **YAML (The Rules):** Clean, readable instructions for how the "Vessel" (Infrastructure) should be built.
5. **TypeScript (The Shield):** Reliable code for the UI that catches mistakes before they reach the user.
6. **npm (The Library):** The massive supply chain of ready-to-use tools and frameworks.
7. **Protobuf (The Speed):** A high-performance, machine-only language for agents to talk at 100x speed.

### The Loop (How it Connects)
**Data is Born (SQL)** → **Processed (Python)** → **Packaged (JSON)** → **Displayed (TypeScript)** → **Interacted with (User/LLM)** → **Verified (SQL)**.
