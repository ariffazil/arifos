## 🔬 Metabolic Theory: The Entropy Tax

arifOS treats AI intelligence as a **Metabolic Process** that consumes thermodynamic resources. 

1.  **Entropy Reduction**: An AI that generates 1000 lines of code without explaining *why* is increasing the entropy of the system. arifOS forces the AI to "pay" for this by providing a clarity-delta ($\Delta S$).
2.  **Landauer’s Bound**: Erasing information costs energy. We treat the "forgetting" of context as a thermodynamic loss, favoring agents that maintain a tight, grounded history.
3.  **Hysteresis**: We calculate the "memory of the system"—ensuring that today's decisions are consistent with the long-term sovereign intent.
