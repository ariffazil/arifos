# Introduction to arifOS

Welcome to the arifOS Documentation.

**arifOS** is one of the first open-source **Constitutional Kernels** for artificial intelligence. It governs whether AI cognition is permitted by mathematical enforcement of ethical constraints.

## The Intelligence Kernel
Unlike simple wrappers, arifOS acts as a cognition controller:
- **Existence Control**: Permits or denies execution based on 13 floors.
- **Resource Allocation**: Monitors thermodynamic cost.
- **Isolation**: Manages sandboxed AI containers.

## Trinity Architecture
arifOS operates through three core engines:
1. **AGI (Mind)**: Cognition, sensing, and reasoning.
2. **ASI (Heart)**: Safety, empathy, and stakeholder impact.
3. **APEX (Soul)**: Judicial synthesis, consensus, and final verdicts.

## VAULT999
Every decision is recorded in **VAULT999**, an immutable Merkle-chained PostgreSQL ledger.
