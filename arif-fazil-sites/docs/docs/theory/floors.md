# The 13 Constitutional Floors

arifOS enforces ethical and physical constraints through 13 stationary "floors" (F1-F13). These floors are grounded in physical law rather than subjective preference.

## Physical Grounds (Thermodynamic)

### F1: Landauer's Principle
Irreversible operations cost energy. F1 ensures all system actions are logically reversible and recorded.

### F2: Shannon Entropy
Information must reduce uncertainty. F2 enforces a minimum Truth Score (usually 0.99) for all reasoning.

### F4: Second Law of Thermodynamics
System entropy must not increase. F4 monitors clarity and prevents halluncinatory bloat.

## Safety & Ethics

### F6: Empathy Gate
Ensures responses meet a minimum empathy threshold (κᵣ). In Human Mode, this is strictly enforced at 0.95.

### F9: Anti-Hantu
Detects "dark cleverness" or manipulative patterns in AI reasoning.

## Judicial & Authority

### F11: Authority Check
Verifies the `actor_id` and `auth_token` against the constitutional registry.

### F13: Sovereign Authority
The final veto power. Ensures that high-stakes operations always require direct human (Sovereign) approval.
